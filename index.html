Methodology

Product design workflow
built for institutional environments

Structured, evidence-driven, and designed to build institutional confidence at each phase — without sacrificing creative problem-solving or design quality.

01
Discovery

Stakeholder mapping, existing service audit, legislative constraints review, and project scope definition.

02
Research

Contextual inquiry, expert interviews, co-design workshops, heuristic evaluation, and analytics review.

03
Systems Mapping

Service blueprinting, information architecture, user journey mapping, and integration dependencies.

04
UX Strategy

Experience principles, design criteria, accessibility targets, and phased delivery roadmap.

05
Prototyping

Progressive fidelity from concept wireframes through interactive Figma prototypes ready for testing.

06
Validation

Moderated usability testing with representative users, accessibility audits, and stakeholder review cycles.

07
Design System

Component library, token documentation, Storybook integration, algorithm creation and maintenance, and developer handoff preparation.

08
Delivery & Governance

Implementation support, QA review, post-launch measurement, and ongoing design stewardship.

About

Strategic, systematic,
and grounded in real institutions

Certified Web Accessibility Expert (ISO/IEC 17024:2012)
Mastery of accessible web design guidelines and techniques — advising and delivering web projects with usability and usable accessibility at the core.
MAS in App Development, FH Technikum Vienna
Grounded in design research, systems thinking, and critical theory alongside craft and practice.
Experienced in managing interdisciplinary projects and groups
Building alignment across policy, technical, legal, and communications teams — from project kickoff through delivery and governance.

For over sixteen years I have led UX strategy and design at the intersection of public-sector policy, enterprise technology, and human-centred service delivery. My work spans federal ministries, agricultural authorities, transport operators, and entertainment — organisations where design decisions affect millions of people and must withstand legal and accessibility scrutiny.

I specialise in bringing clarity to complex systems — whether that means restructuring a national farmer services portal serving 400,000 users, or designing an offline-first mobile app for farmers in remote alpine regions. I believe that the best institutional digital services are invisible: they simply work, for everyone.
